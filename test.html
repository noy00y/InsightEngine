â€‹Automated Creditor Insurance Questionnaire
  • â€‹When a client agrees to apply for a creditor insurance product, they see a Creditor Insurance Questionnaire after the Recommendation screen
    • The questionnaire helps with the insurance discussion, giving clients more details about the product and coverage
    • The client's responses on the questionnaire will help decide whether the client is eligible for insurance coverage
  • Clients must complete a questionnaire for every insurance product they apply for
  • Once the client completes the insurance questionnaire, if they are no longer eligible for the insurance product, they see a notification box telling them to click Remove before continuing
  • Once you go back to the Offer Recommendations screen, you only see the remaining eligible insurance products

eSignature (using the Wacom device)
  • If a client has been set up to sign and receive documents electronically, the product origination documents are generated on Compass and displayed on the eSignature device attached to your workstation
  • The advisor and the clients use the eSignature device to review the documents, and then use the stylus to sign and initial the forms
  • Documents are electronically delivered and stored in the client's digital vault
  • Bank copies of eSigned documents are automatically stored and imaged. You can retrieve the documents from Document Search on ECIF
